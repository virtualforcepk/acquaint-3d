Case study · Lead Gen · Real estate

88,000 prospects.

Lease At Ease is a property-management business serving owner-landlords in Ontario. We built their entire lead-generation engine — and we run it for them, every single day.

Build yoursHow lead gen works
The problem

Their next customer was invisible — and expensive to find.

Owner-landlords don't gather in one place. Finding them means combing listings, classifieds, and public records at a scale no human team can sustain — and every day a prospect sits untouched, they sign with someone else. Manual prospecting produced a trickle; the business needed a pipeline.

What we built

One machine. Four moving parts.

01 · Targeting

Source at scale

Automated sourcing across the places their prospects actually appear — listings, ads, and public data — deduplicated into one clean pool.

02 · AI scoring

Rank every lead

Every prospect is scored and graded by fit signals, so outreach time goes to the leads most likely to convert — not the loudest ones.

03 · Outreach

Multi-channel contact

Automated first-touch outreach across channels, paced and governed, with every send logged and deduplicated against contact history.

04 · Follow-up

Nothing goes cold

Replies are classified, warm leads escalate to a human, and every contact lands in the CRM with full history — daily, without being asked.
