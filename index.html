Questions, answered

The things people ask first.

How much does a website cost?

From $97/month plus tax, with a one-time setup quoted after your call. Hosting, weekly site updates, SEO, backlink building, and a monthly strategy session are included. Prefer to own it outright? A one-time flat fee covers the whole build and you manage the code and design yourself.

What comes after the website?

Custom lead generation, run as a consulting engagement and scoped to your business. Many clients start with the website and add lead gen when they're ready to grow.

How does the lead-gen system work?

Two motions run as one system. Outbound: AI agents handle targeting, multi-channel outreach, lead-scoring, and automated follow-up to find your next customer. Inbound: an AI receptionist with CRM integration and custom automations answers, books, and qualifies every call 24/7. We build it and run it — you get the booked calls.

Where is Acquaint Media located?

Mississauga, Ontario — serving businesses across Canada.

How fast can my site go live?

Typically under 3 weeks from kick-off: discovery and strategy, then design and build, then launch — and we maintain it weekly after that. Depending on the complexity of the build, turnaround can be under 24 hours.
