The founder

From the sales floor to the machine room.

Kamran Butt started where the money is actually made — the luxury sales floor. Two years at Burberry ($3M+ sold), then Moncler, where he built a private 1,500-client WeChat channel and drove $2M+ in clienteling. The lesson stuck: great selling isn't charisma, it's a system — the right person, the right message, followed up without fail.

So he started building the systems. Growth and media buying for Ricco Decor, Lamar Donair, and Jawanda Consulting. A CASL-compliant acquisition machine for Lease At Ease. Then a fully autonomous, multi-agent AI pipeline that sources and qualifies hundreds of companies and sends 300+ personalised emails a day — built and run solo.

Today he builds those same machines for Canadian small businesses. No dashboard to babysit, no learning curve — he builds the system, runs it, and hands you booked calls. He doesn't sell the demo. He is the demo.
